Differences between crochet and knitting

Most crochet uses one hook and works upon one stitch at a time. Crochet may be worked in circular rounds without any specialized tools, as shown here. Knitting uses two or more straight needles that carry multiple stitches.Unlike crochet, knitting requires specialized needles to create circular rounds.
